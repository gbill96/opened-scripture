Our Mission

Every issue anchored in God's Word

Opened Scripture exists to help believers think Biblically — not just on Sunday, but every day of the week. We bring theology out of the classroom and into the kitchen table, the office, the ballot box, and the living room.

Each issue takes on real-life topics — culture, family, work, politics, anxiety, relationships — and filters them through the unchanging truth of Scripture.

  • Scripture-rooted, not opinion-driven
  • Gospel-centered in every issue
  • Doctrinally sound and practical
  • Written for real life, not just Sunday
  • Free to all believers, always
"All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness."
2 Timothy 3:16
We believe the Bible is not a collection of ancient wisdom — it is the living Word of God, sufficient for every situation, culture, and century. Opened Scripture helps you apply it daily.
